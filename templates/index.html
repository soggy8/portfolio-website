About

I'm a passionate full-stack developer and electronics enthusiast who started coding and building websites at the age of 10.

With 7+ years of self-taught experience and 3 months of professional work, I've developed a deep understanding of both software and hardware. I specialize in web development with Python, Flask, and FastAPI, while also working extensively with embedded systems like Arduino and ESP32.

My journey has been marked by achievements including winning a national hackathon, earning a full scholarship to FINKI in North Macedonia, and taking first place in the national Kengur competition for logical mathematical thinking.

Beyond web development, I'm passionate about electronics, digital systems, and IoT projects that bridge the gap between software and the physical world.
